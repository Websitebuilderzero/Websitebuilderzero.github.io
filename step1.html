Stage 1

Looking for the best site-maker

To create a good site, you need to find no less good program to create this site. Here I will try to disassemble three site-makers and choose the best (at least for myself). Let's start.
Wix:
* As many as 6 different subscriptions!
* Free - version (Free domain, free hosting, 500MB of space on the server, 1GB of some Bandwidth, I have no idea what it is)
+ Optimized for different devices
+ You can track the number of visitors to the site
+ Free Hosting
+ Quite democratic prices for subscriptions
- On the site created with the free version, will be advertising Wix.
- A large number of different subscriptions, therefore, Free - version is heavily truncated relative to paid
Now Weebly:
* Four types of subscriptions (+ four more for online stores)
* Free - subscription (see screenshot)
+ Bright, colorful site
+ A great website-maker for small businesses or online stores.
+ Again advertising in the Free version.
- Expensive Subscriptions
Well, in the end Mobirise:
+ The whole site is intuitive and understandable.
+ Optimization for different devices
+ The most informative site of all
+ Are there no paid subscriptions (on the site I have not found it anywhere)?
+ User Reviews
+ Endorsed by critics (there's even Bloomberg there)
-You should download the program
-It is not at all clear on the exact figures (what count in the site's MB can be stored, what is the maximum number of pages the site can have).
